LOT1 NEW | ART | AUCTIONS

LOT1 is a Berlin-based startup that wants to make art auctions more accessible for all. They aim to showcase young and upcoming Berlin artists' works via object recognition and AR software all integrated in one single app. This case study is about my collaboration with the founders, a 3-day design thinking workshop and the ongoing processes.

Feel free to browse this site and if any questions arise, do not hesitate to contact me. Enjoy!
