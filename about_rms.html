About

I'm Maija. UX Designer.

But also teacher trainer, CrossFitter, book lover, nature enthusiast and I think we all need better apps in our lives.

My Story

I'm a UX designer with a background in teaching secondary school and mentoring teacher training workshops about Gender & Diversity. I'm looking to further my career by working as a UX designer in the fields of language learning/teaching or fitness/sports as these comprise my passion and expertise.

After some time in the teaching position, I wanted to focus more on the benefits of digitalization in the classroom and sports and scale my abilities and experience to make learning simpler and more attractive as well as accessible.

By using my background in education, my creativity and passion for learning & exercise, I want to build simple yet satisfying user experiences and product solutions.
